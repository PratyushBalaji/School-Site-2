Basketball

With 2 courts for basketball and expert coaches from around India, students learn any new techniques and frequently go for tournaments in other schools sucha as TISB, Stonehill, entrance


Athletics

An 8 lane 200m track, Outdoor rope climbing, pole carrying, climbing nets, monkey bars and a long jump area with sand pit, we at Neev pride ourselves on our athletics team. Students go through vigorous training session to achieve their goals in athletics


Football

Our football field doubles as 2 smaller ones for all our students to use. Whether is a playful match or an intense tournament, our football coaches ensure that our students are at the top of their game.


Tennis

With 6 tennis courts and a batch of hard working students, we try our level best to drive the students forward and crush their opponents. Our tennis team strives to defeat an win every match.


Swimming

Our olympic sized swimming pool and team of highly experienced coaches have a lot to offer to the swimming team. We frequently host inter-house sports competitions and swimming seems to be the most intense. Neev has secured many medals in swimming across schools and will continue to do so in the future.
